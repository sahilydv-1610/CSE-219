Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase tickets?

You can purchase tickets directly through our website by selecting the event you're interested in and following the checkout process. We accept all major credit cards and PayPal.

What is your refund policy?

Refunds are available up to 48 hours before the event. After that point, all sales are final. To request a refund, please contact our support team with your order details.

How do I access my tickets after purchase?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email confirmation with your tickets attached as PDFs. You can also access your tickets anytime by logging into your account on our website.

What if an event is canceled?

In the event of a cancellation, we will automatically issue refunds to all ticket holders. You'll receive an email notification with details about the cancellation and refund process.

Can I transfer my tickets to someone else?

Yes, most tickets can be transferred. Log into your account, go to "My Tickets," and select the transfer option. You'll need the recipient's email address to complete the transfer.
